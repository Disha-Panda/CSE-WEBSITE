HOD's Message

The Department of Computer Science Engineering offers a comprehensive curriculum covering core computer science and engineering, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, and software engineering. Students are encouraged to work on real-world projects, engage in research activities, and participate in internships and hackathons. The faculty is experienced and knowledgeable, ensuring students can adapt and excel in new environments and technologies to solve real-world problems.

About our
Institution

Inaugurated in the year of 2016, Ramkrishna Mahato Government Engineering College (Formerly known as Purulia Government Engineering College) is fully funded by the Government of West Bengal and is approved by AICTE. Affiliated to MAKAUT (formerly known as WBUT), the college at its nascent stage comprises five academic departments like Electronics & Communication Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering, and is well equipped with state of arts laboratories and workshops. Situated at Agharpur.
